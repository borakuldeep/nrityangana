Welcome to NK Dance School

Your gateway to the vibrant and captivating world of Indian dance and culture right here in the US. We celebrate the rich tapestry of culture and unleash the vibrant talents of our local community. We take pride in nurturing a sense of community and fostering cross-culture exchange.

Cultural events

NK dance school participates regularly in cultural events, performances, competitions that serve as platforms for our students ti express themselves, share their love for Indian Culture and build bridges of understanding with the wider community.

Traditional values

Our School also embraces the concept of 'Guru-Shishya parampara' which emphasises the bond between teacher and student. This traditional approach ensures personalised attention, mentorship and nurturing environment where students can grow not only as dancers but also as individuals. We believe in instilling discipline, dedication and a deep appreciation for Indian heritage with in each student.

Wether you are a beginner or an experienced dancer, a child or an adult, Nrityakalpna Dance School welcomes you to embark on a transformative journey of self- discovery, cultural exploration and artistic expressions. Let us be your cultural bridge, connecting you to the rich traditions and artistic treasures of India.
